Credit Risk Scoring

This demo scores loan applications using income, debt burden, credit score, and loan terms — the same inputs underwriting teams already review. It estimates payback vs. default risk to support early screening, not automated credit decisions.

  • Instant payback and default probabilities for each application
  • Validated against simple policy rules and stronger benchmarks
  • Trained on ~594,000 historical loan records
